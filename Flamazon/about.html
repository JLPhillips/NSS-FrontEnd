About Us!

We are a very dedicated, flamingo merchandise store, open since 1994 in downtown Flamingo, Florida! We cater to flamingo fans of every type! From the fledgling flamingo friendly, to the frantic flamingo fanatic, we have it all! Get one of our award winning hats or shirts, or join the upper crust of flamingo devotion with your very own synthetic yard flamingo! Give us a call and we can help set you up with just as much flamingoness as a person can handle! So come on down to our physical shop or buy some of our wares online! We hope you enjoy your visit either way and have a flamazonian day!
